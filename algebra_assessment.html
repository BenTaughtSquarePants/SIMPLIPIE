Pre-assessment test

1. Define the term "quadratic equation."
2. An equation with a single variable raised to the power of three.
3. Solve for x: 2x + 5 = 11.
4. Find the value of y in the equation 4y - 9 = 7.
5. What is the additive inverse of a + b?
6. Solve for x: 3(x − 2) = 15.
7. Simplify the expression 3(2x - 4) + 7x.
8. What is the solution to the system of equations 2x - y = 4 and 3x + 2y = 1?
9. Factorize the quadratic expression x2 - 5x + 6.
10. Solve the inequality 2x + 7 < 15.
