Algebra

Learning algebra involves acquiring the ability to manipulate mathematical symbols and expressions, solve equations, and understand the relationships and patterns inherent in numerical and variable representations.

Geometry

Learning geometry involves acquiring the skills to analyze and comprehend the properties, measurements, and relationships of shapes and spaces, encompassing principles governing angles, distances, and various geometric figures.

Trigonometry

Learning trigonometry involves understanding the relationships between angles and sides in triangles, exploring the properties of trigonometric functions, and applying these concepts to solve problems related to triangles and periodic phenomena.

Pre Calculus

Learning Pre Calculus involves mastering advanced algebraic concepts, trigonometry, and mathematical functions, providing a comprehensive foundation for calculus by delving into topics like polynomial and rational functions, exponential and logarithmic functions, and analytical geometry.

Basic Calculus

Learning Basic Calculus involves understanding the fundamental principles of differentiation and integration, enabling the analysis of rates of change, slopes of curves, and the accumulation of quantities, laying the groundwork for more advanced mathematical applications in various fields.

Calculus

Learning Calculus entails grasping foundational concepts such as limits, derivatives, and integrals, providing a deep understanding of rates of change, slopes, and accumulated quantities, and serving as a crucial framework for tackling intricate problem-solving scenarios across diverse disciplines like physics, engineering, and economics.
